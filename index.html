How to learn the drums!

Step 1: Try to get a clear understanding of the rhythms

By doing this you need to have a good tempo and its usually best to stick with that speed(tempo) The tempo is '1, 2, 3, 4'. All drummers most have a tempo without a tempo the rhythm will be out of sync.

There's this item that's called a metronome you can set a temp from 4 to 32 beats. For beginners I would recommended it. It help me get on track and get me in the mindset on how everything works.

Timing is Key!

Step 2: Introducing the Snare and Bass

The bass drum is played on beats 1 and 3 and the snare drum is played on beats 2 and 4.

To play the snare you use your left hand and for the bass you use your right foot.

The snare is the first drum you hear on the track
The bass is the secound drum you hear on the track

Step 3: Learning the basic with the snare and bass

This video uses 8 beats which you learn how to play the high hat with the snare and bass.

After mastering these three instruments you'll be able to play almost any beats.
